This is a simple demo application showing the possibilities of Linked Data. Information on a address is searched in a triple-store via SPARQL. The building connected to that address is shown on the map. The application looks for statistical information on the area of the building. This information is obtained from Statistics Netherlands. One of the statistical information is about the average number of bars within a 1 km radius. To make it more interesting: we use OpenStreetMap to tell us the location of the pubs and bars

LinkedData Example

Enter a zipcode and a house number and click the search button. A linked data a store is now searched for this address. The building is shown on the map and more information about the area is presented as well. It can take up to 10-15 seconds before an awnser appears. The cars and pubs around the area are indicated on the map.
